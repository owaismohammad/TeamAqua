AquaHarvest: Rainwater Calculator

The AquaHarvest rainwater harvesting calculator plays a vital role in educating people about water conservation by providing them with practical insights and tools to understand the importance of utilizing rainwater as a valuable resource. By using the calculator, individuals can visualize the potential benefits of rainwater harvesting systems in their own homes or communities. They can explore various scenarios and see firsthand how collecting rainwater can contribute to reducing water consumption, conserving freshwater resources, and mitigating the impact of droughts.
