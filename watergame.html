About the Game

Join our brave fish friend on an underwater adventure in 'Aqua Escape: Swim for Survival'! Dive deep into the ocean as you swim through a world of challenges and obstacles. Your mission? To gobble up as many food particles as you can while the water level steadily drops. But be careful! With each passing moment, the water gets lower and lower, making it harder to swim. Can you help the fish survive in this shrinking sea? Test your skills, score points, and learn about the importance of saving water in this exciting underwaterÂ journey!

How to Play

  1. Use the Arrow keys to control the fish.
  2. Guide the fish to eat the food particles to score points.
  3. Avoid going out of bounds or running out of water.
  4. Click anywhere outside the modal to close it.
